# Marketing-Campaign
## 📊 Power BI Business Intelligence Project
## A Power BI business intelligence project analyzing multi-channel marketing performance, customer retention, and database demand.

## Project Overview
-This project delivers an interactive marketing analytics dashboard for Marketing Campaign ROI & Conversion Dataset to track engagement, conversions, and client retention across WhatsApp, SMS, Email, and Meta API campaigns.​
-The dashboard helps marketing leaders quickly identify high-performing channels, cities, industries, and at-risk customer segments.

### 🎯 Business Objective  
The goal is to enable data-driven marketing decisions by answering:

-Which channels generate the highest engagement and ROI?

-Which cities and industries show the highest marketing database demand?

-What percentage of clients return for repeated marketing services?

-Which client groups are at risk of churn?

-How does messaging volume relate to conversions across the funnel?

### 📁 Dataset  
- 5,000+ marketing campaign records  
- 128M+ messages sent via WhatsApp, SMS, Email, and Meta API  
- Segmentation by target city and industry for granular analysis

### 🧠 Key Metrics (KPIs)
- Delivery rate
- Open rate and click-through rate
- Conversion funnel (sent → delivered → engaged → converted)
- Repeat client rate
- Customer lifetime value (LTV) and churn risk score  

### 📌 Key Insights
- WhatsApp & Meta API generate highest engagement  
- Metro cities show highest marketing database usage  
- Coaching & Healthcare industries lead demand  
- Repeat clients drive higher lifetime value  

### 🛠 Tech Stack  
-Power BI (reporting and visualization)

-DAX (calculated measures and KPIs)

-Excel / CSV (source data)

-ETL and data modeling for star-schema reporting

### 📈 Key Insights

WhatsApp and Meta API emerge as the strongest performing marketing channels in terms of engagement and conversions.

Metro cities such as Hyderabad, Kolkata, Bangalore, Chennai, and Pune show the highest marketing demand and activity.

Coaching, Healthcare, and Retail are the top revenue-generating industries for marketing campaigns.

Repeat clients contribute approximately 3–5x more revenue compared to new clients, highlighting the value of retention.

Overall database size shows a strong positive relationship with messaging volume and conversion outcomes.
